DONATION REQUEST GUIDELINES

Thank you for considering us as a potential contributor to your organization. We are proud to support initiatives that uplift our community through education, charity, and local events. In order to fairly evaluate requests, we kindly ask that you complete the donation request form below. Please note the following guidelines:

Donation Requests

ELIGIBILITY

We prioritize donations to registered non-profit organizations, schools, and charitable events. Proof of your organization's non-profit status may be required.

SUBMISSION

All requests must be submitted at least 30 days prior to the date of the event. We are unable to accommodate last-minute requests.

SCOPE

While we wish we could support every request, donations will be granted based on the alignment of your cause with our mission and the availability of resources.

RESPONSE TIME

Due to the volume of requests, please allow up to two weeks for a response. Submission of a request does not guarantee approval.

We greatly appreciate the work you do for our community, and we look forward to reviewing your application. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
